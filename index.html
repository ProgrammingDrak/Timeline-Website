List the Property

If none of the offers are appealing to the Seller, they can move forward with 7 Day Sold. The Seller may restrict offers to cash depending upon how fast they want to move, but you can make it clear to the Seller that the more restrictive the listing, the smaller the buyer pool and that could affect the purchase price. For that reason they may choose to extend the timeline or ultimately switch to a traditional listing.
